We are ecoBank. Our goal is to reduce carbon emissions and global warming. This company was started in mid-2023 by Kingston Mariano. Our first product, the ecoBank 1, had some success. Our company is now currently worth $700,000. Our plan is to introduce people of all nationalities to our products. The ecoBank 1 helps combat climate change caused by humans, such as global warming. It also helps battle things like deforestation and plastic waste. The ecoBank 1 also has a lot of unique features like solar panels and speakers. Our products are meant for anyone 12 years and older.
